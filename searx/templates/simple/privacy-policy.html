Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Latest2All Search. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our search engine. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. What We Collect

We DO NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Search queries (we don't log what you search for)
  • IP addresses (we don't store them)
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Browser fingerprinting data

We may temporarily collect for rate limiting:

  • IP addresses (automatically deleted after 24 hours)
  • Request frequency (to prevent abuse)

3. How We Protect Your Privacy

  • No tracking: We don't track your searches or browsing history
  • No personalization: Search results are not personalized based on your history
  • HTTPS encryption: All connections are encrypted
  • No third-party tracking: We don't share data with advertisers
  • Open source: Our code is open for inspection

4. Third-Party Services

When you search, we query multiple search engines. These engines may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies if you have concerns.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our service without providing personal information
  • Request deletion of any temporary data we may have
  • Use our service anonymously

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website: https://latest2all.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
