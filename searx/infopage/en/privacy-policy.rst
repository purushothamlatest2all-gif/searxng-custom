Privacy Policy
==============

Latest2All Search is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect
---------------

**We DO collect:**

- **Aggregate search statistics**: Total search counts and engine usage for service improvement
- **Search queries**: Temporarily processed to provide results, not permanently stored
- **Error logs**: Technical errors for debugging (no personal data)

**We DO NOT collect:**

- IP addresses (not logged)
- User agents (not logged)
- Personal identifiers (names, emails, etc.)
- Search history (not stored long-term)
- Cookies for tracking purposes

How We Use Your Data
--------------------

1. **Search Processing**: Your query is sent to search engines to retrieve results
2. **Statistics**: Aggregate counts help us improve the service
3. **Error Handling**: Technical issues are logged for debugging

Third-Party Services
--------------------

- Search results are fetched from various search engines (Brave, DuckDuckGo, Wikipedia, etc.)
- Each search engine has its own privacy policy
- No tracking cookies are set by our instance
- No advertising or monetization

Your Rights
-----------

- **No account required**: Use the service anonymously
- **No data retention**: Queries are not stored permanently
- **Opt-out**: Simply stop using the service

Changes to This Policy
